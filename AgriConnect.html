Buy Fresh. Sell Direct. Empower Farmers.

Welcome to AgriConnect, Kenya's first all-inclusive digital marketplace designed to connect farmers directly with customers. Whether you're a small-scale farmer selling vegetables or a large-scale producer of cereals, AgriConnect gives you the power to reach buyers instantly — without middlemen.

Why Choose AgriConnect?

At AgriConnect, we are building more than just an online store. We are creating a trusted community where farmers and customers work together to grow Kenya's food economy.

Farmers List Products

Every farmer gets a personal account to showcase their products — from fresh maize and vegetables to livestock and dairy. You control your prices and reach customers directly.

Customers Buy Easily

Customers browse a wide range of fresh produce, compare prices, and order from the comfort of their homes. Delivery is fast, reliable, and affordable.

Chat & Negotiate

Farmers and customers can chat directly on the platform. Whether you want to negotiate bulk prices or ask about product quality, our chat system keeps everything transparent.

Trusted Feedback

After every purchase, customers leave reviews. This builds trust and helps farmers improve their service while rewarding those who deliver quality.

Our Story

Kenya's farmers feed the nation, but many still struggle to sell their produce fairly.

Middlemen take large cuts, customers face high prices, and fresh produce often goes to waste.

AgriConnect was born out of a mission to change this. We believe in a Kenya where:

  • Farmers earn fairly for their hard work.
  • Customers enjoy affordable fresh food.
  • Technology bridges the gap between the two.

Our vision is to build Africa's most trusted farmers' marketplace — starting right here in Kenya.
